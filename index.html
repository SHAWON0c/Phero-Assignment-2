Healthy Diet Flowchart

Follow this simple flowchart to understand the basic principles of a balanced diet.

Start

Plan Your Meals

Planning your meals in advance helps you make healthier food choices throughout the week.

Step 1

Include Fruits & Vegetables

Adding fruits and vegetables to your diet boosts your intake of essential vitamins and minerals.

Step 2

Choose Whole Grains

Whole grains provide fiber and nutrients, supporting digestive health and long-lasting energy.

Step 3

Pick Lean Proteins

Lean proteins help build and repair tissues while keeping you feeling full and satisfied.

Step 4

Drink Plenty of Water

Drinking enough water is vital for hydration, energy, and overall health.

Finish

Enjoy a Balanced Diet

Maintaining a balanced diet ensures you receive all the essential nutrients your body needs.
